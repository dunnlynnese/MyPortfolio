Glitch and Mash-up

The cake glitch was supposed to be a little disturbing with it's imagery. I think of sweet things when I think of desserts so I wanted to use a cookie, s'mores and a cake for the focus of the glitch. Since we are supposed to change the meaning of the image I wanted to glitch the desserts together to make them seem gross or disturbing. The discoloration of the pink with the background blending the three desserts together really took away some of the sweet imagery that desserts give me.

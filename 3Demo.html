3D printing that represent an emotion

This project asked us to focus on 3 Dimensional renderings that was made from an emotion. My group was called the Ravens and we decided to focus on renderings that were inspired by the feeling sadness. We individually came up with our own sadness symbols. My symbol is this black heart. The heart itself is supposed to be a dark color to signify it's emptiness.

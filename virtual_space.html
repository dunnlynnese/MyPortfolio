3D model and Virtual Space

For this assignment I wanted to great something more abstract. For this I decided I would create a colorful ball that reminded me of royalty. The purple and gold are considered royal colors and the green was a good contrast to both. I wanted to create more angular shapes with in the sphere so it could have more shape and depth. This allowed the top of the ball to create a sun shape that emphasizes the gold and the ripple of purple and green colors the follows.
